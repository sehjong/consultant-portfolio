My Services

Knowing what to expect when we work together is critical - here is my process:

  1. Consultation - We'll talk about your needs and your budget. It all begins with a conversation where we'll pinpoint what this project is all about.
  2. Proposal & Contract - I'll draw up a proposal and contract. A detailed project proposal will be presented and a plan of action will be decided. You'll review, and only sign if/when you are comfortable.
  3. Creation - Once deposit is received, the work begins and I'll keep you informed with regular updates.
  4. Launch - Once full payment is submitted, the site is yours.
